Esq Drucil E. Taylor. Legal

Drucil E. Taylor is a respected Barrister at Law renowned for his expertise in mining law and anti-corruption practices. He has been instrumental in drafting key legislation in Sierra Leone, including the Mines and Mineral Act 2009 and the Anti-Corruption Act 2008. Drucil has participated in prestigious programs on International Humanitarian Law and International Criminal Law, enhancing his global perspective and legal acumen. His proficiency extends to negotiating international trade disputes, making him a sought-after advisor for international mining companies, venture capitalists, and industrial asset providers in Sierra Leone.

In addition to his legal contributions, Drucil has made significant strides in shaping legal discourse within the mining sector. His work on the International Comparative Legal Guide on Mining Law - Sierra Leone 2015 reflects his commitment to excellence and innovation in the field. Drucil E. Taylor is widely respected for his dedication to legal integrity and his invaluable contributions to the legal community.

Mr Joshua Bonney (ACII)Chief Executive Officer

Joshua Bonney (ACII), a Chartered Insurance Practitioner with over two decades of experience in the insurance industry, is the team leader and Chief Executive Officer at Core Insurance Solutions Limited. The Chartered Insurance Institute in London has accredited him as a Chartered Insurance Practitioner. Joshua holds a Master of Science in Actuarial Science from Heriot-Watt University, Edinburgh, and a Bachelor of Education in Mathematics from the University of Cape Coast, Ghana.

With a wealth of experience, Joshua specializes in Property Damage, Fiduciary, Mining, Energy, Marine, Construction, and Engineering insurances. He possesses a profound understanding of the distinct risks associated with each industry, assisting clients in effectively managing and reducing these risks. Joshua, known for his client-centric approach, always puts his clients' best interests first. Joshua's meticulous attention to detail and exceptional analytical abilities have played a pivotal role in restructuring insurance programs for numerous clients. Notably, he has significantly improved the insurance portfolios of prominent clients such as Vimetco, Ecobank SL Ltd., Zenith Bank SL Ltd., Mano Mining, and NP Sierra Leone. His strategic approach has resulted in substantial enhancements to their insurance arrangements, ensuring they receive optimal coverage tailored to their specific requirements.

Joshua has successfully secured substantial claims for both individual and corporate clients, including Sierra Rutile, Vimetco, Ecobank Microfinance, and Orange Sierra Leone.
